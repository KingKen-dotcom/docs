# STEP 1: Create Google Form

## Objective
Create a form that collects worker and employer applications with all necessary information.

## Time Required: 15 minutes

## Setup Instructions

### Part A: Create Blank Form

1. Go to: **forms.google.com**
2. Click **"Blank Form"** (plus icon)
3. You'll be taken to new form editor

### Part B: Add Title & Description

**Title:** 
```
Kingken Global Recruitment Application Form
```

**Description:**
```
Apply for overseas job opportunities with Kingken Global Travel Agency Ltd.

We connect verified African workers with employers in Kuwait, UAE, and Qatar.

Fill this form completely and upload your CV. Our team will review and contact you.
```

### Part C: Add Questions (CRITICAL)

**Question 1: Full Name**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes
- Help text: "Enter your full name as shown in your passport"

**Question 2: Phone Number**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes
- Help text: "Include country code (e.g., +256701234567)"

**Question 3: Nationality**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes
- Help text: "Your country of origin"

**Question 4: Position Applying For**
- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes
- Options:
  - Cleaner
  - Domestic Helper
  - Construction Worker
  - Logistics/Driver
  - Restaurant Staff
  - Healthcare Worker
  - Other (please specify)

**Question 5: Years of Experience**
- Type: Short answer
- Required: Yes
- Help text: "Number of years in your field"

**Question 6: Passport Available?**
- Type: Multiple choice
- Required: Yes
- Options:
  - Yes, valid and ready
  - Yes, but expired
  - Currently processing
  - No passport yet

**Question 7: Upload CV (File Upload)**
- Type: File upload
- Required: Yes
- Help text: "PDF or Word document only. Max 5MB"

**Question 8 (Optional): Additional Information**
- Type: Paragraph
- Required: No
- Help text: "Any other information you'd like us to know?"

## Form Settings

### Access & Responses
1. Click **Settings** (gear icon, top right)
2. Go to **"Responses"** tab
3. Set: **Collect email addresses**: OFF (optional)
4. Set: **Limit to 1 response**: OFF
5. Set: **Make this a quiz**: OFF

### Confirmation Message
1. Click **Settings**
2. Under **Presentation**, set custom confirmation:

```
Thank you for applying with Kingken Global!

Your application has been received and is under review.

Our team will contact you shortly with updates.

Best regards,
Kingken Global Travel Agency Ltd
```

## Form URL

Once created, you'll have a shareable URL:
```
https://forms.google.com/share/YOUR_FORM_ID
```

Copy this URL for later use in:
- Website
- Marketing ads
- WhatsApp messages
- Email campaigns

## Common Issues & Solutions

**Issue:** Form won't load
- Solution: Clear browser cache, try different browser

**Issue:** File upload not working
- Solution: Check file size (max 5MB), try PDF format

**Issue:** Can't find Settings
- Solution: Click the **gear icon** in top right corner

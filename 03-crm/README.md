## What is a CRM?

A CRM stores and organizes:
- ✅ All candidate information
- ✅ All employer information
- ✅ Job requests and openings
- ✅ Sales pipeline (deal stages)
- ✅ Revenue tracking
- ✅ Team tasks
- ✅ Communication history

## Quick Setup (30 minutes)

### Step 1: Create Google Sheet
1. sheets.google.com → Create new
2. Name: "Kingken Global CRM System"
3. Share with team

### Step 2: Create 8 Sheets
1. Right-click sheet tab
2. Insert sheet
3. Create: Dashboard, Candidates, Employers, Job Requests, Pipeline, Deals & Revenue, Tasks, Contacts
